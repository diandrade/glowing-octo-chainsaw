Benvenuti all'Hotel Modica Boutique

About

The Modica Boutique Hotel is a modern 4-star hotel where attention to detail and unique design leave plenty of scope for functionality. A harmonious design with absolutely unconventional characteristics. Sophisticated furnishing elements, from the world of architecture or art, will be the perfect setting for your business trips, romantic escapes and relaxing family trips.

How to Find

  • From Catania (Airport or City): Take A18/E45 highway toward Siracusa.
  • Merge onto SS114 → SS115 (direction Ragusa/Modica).
  • Exit at "Variante SS115 21/D" – the hotel is near this exit.
